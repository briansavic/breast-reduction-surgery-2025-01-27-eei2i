Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recovery time for breast reduction surgery?

Recovery typically takes 4-6 weeks, with most patients returning to normal activities within 2-3 weeks.

Will the surgery leave scars?

While some scarring is inevitable, our surgical techniques minimize visible scarring, and scars typically fade over time.

Is breast reduction covered by insurance?

Coverage varies by provider. We can help you determine if your procedure qualifies for insurance coverage.
