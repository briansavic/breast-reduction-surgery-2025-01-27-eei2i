Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recovery time for breast reduction surgery?

Most patients return to work within 2-3 weeks and resume full activities by 6 weeks. Complete healing continues for several months as swelling subsides.

What type of anesthesia is used?

The procedure is performed under general anesthesia by our certified anesthesiologists, ensuring your comfort and safety throughout the surgery.

Will my insurance cover the procedure?

Many insurance providers cover breast reduction when it's medically necessary. Our team will help you navigate the insurance process and documentation requirements.

How long do results last?

Results are typically permanent, though natural aging and weight fluctuations can affect breast size and shape over time.

What are the potential risks?

While complications are rare, we'll discuss all potential risks during your consultation and provide detailed pre- and post-operative care instructions.
